## About

tawk.to is designed to increase the effectiveness in managing the online customer engagement experience, enabling multiple websites and agents in a single dashboard interface to chat with the visitors on your website.
Compatible with all modern browsers, tawk.to is a free live chat software created out of the growing need for businesses to respond in real time, with real people.

There is no 'premium' version, just one feature packed version for everyone.

## Functionalities

- Real Time Monitoring;
- Automated Triggers;
- Customizable Widgets;
- 27 Languages;
- Mail Notifications.

[See more](https://www.tawk.to/features/#)

## How to set up

1. Install the app from VTEX App Store;
2. After creating a tawk.to account, fill in the Property ID in the app settings;
3. Save your changes.
